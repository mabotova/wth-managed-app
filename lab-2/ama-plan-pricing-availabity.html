Pricing and availability allows us to set the following values for our plan.

  1. The markets in which the plan will be applicable. You may choose 1 or more markets in which to sell this plan.
  2. Pricing - Set a monthly subscription price for your plan. For testing purposes, you may create a plan with a $0 price.
  3. Dimensions of metered billing - Not yet covered in the workshop.
  4. Plan visibility - indicate if this plan is to be made public or restricted to a specific customer. Private plans are often used to provide custom pricing for a given customer.
