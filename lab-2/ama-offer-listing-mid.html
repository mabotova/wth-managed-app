On this screen you may specify the name of the offer as it will be seen by your customers. You may also specify a summary to be shown along with your offer in search results when customers are searching the Azure Marketplace.

Short and long descriptions are also customizable, and will be seen by customers. Alo, specify up to 3 keywords to help you show up well in searching the Azure Marketplace.

Add a link to your company's privacy policy.

Finally, you may add links to your offer, which will appear when a customer looks at your offer.
