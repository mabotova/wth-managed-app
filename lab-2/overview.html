Partner Center Walkthrough 1

Overview

In this lab, you will walk through the Partner Center experience using images. This enables you to experience partner center without having a Microsoft Partner ID or a Partner Center account.

In the walkthrough experience, find the appropriate places in Partner Center to click in order to complete each step.

Tips

  1. Use your browser's zoom feature if the image is too large to display. In addition to accessing zoom in a browser menu, you can use CNTL+Mouse Wheel to zoom, or pinch on a trackpad.
  2. Each image is an image map. You must find the right place on each screen to click in order to proceed.
  3. Notes for each screen, including the goal for that page, will be written at the bottom of each page.
